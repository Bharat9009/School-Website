GD Goenka Public School-CAMPUS

GD Goenka Public School is a Central Board of Secondary Education school with branches in Dhanbad, Baramulla, Varanasi, Anantnag, Meerut, Darbhanga, U.P & Dakshineswar (Bidyayatan sarani), Kolkata . The pre-primary and early years learning programme at CPS is a system where the tenets of Montessori education are scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.
